Foaling and Yard Security

A modular system – what other cameras do you need from the same platform?

At The Gallops is based on a modular system running through the same technology platform where you have full control to access.

Yard security
We provide 24 hour live view monitoring and with “cloud” based recording for up to 30 days.

  • High definition, high resolution.
  • Infared lights for night vision (capable of illuminating objects 30m away).
  • Built in high-quality microphone so you can hear as well as see.
  • Wide angle lens.

Foaling and stable cameras
Our cameras are great in foaling boxes too, all using the same technology platform. There are no restrictions as to how many cameras you can add. What about giving your owners individual access to their horses in the stable? The sky is the limit in terms of the access that you can give and the engagement you create.
